Nanna Gunnars

Performer, Producer, Writer

Icelandic, based in Reykjavik. Specialist in interactive one-on-one performances, public promenades, and physical theatre. Co-founder of Huldufugl. Founder of Reykjavik Fringe Festival. Languages: Icelandic, English, Spanish, Norwegian, Portuguese.

LinkedIn · Instagram

Reykjavik Fringe Festival (2018-2023)

Founded in 2018. Grew to 100+ acts from 25 countries, 250+ performances across 20 venues. Artistic Director for 5 years. Member of Nordic Fringe Network.

Poetry Brothel Reykjavik / Rauda Skaldahusid (2017-2019)

Immersive poetry cabaret. Nanna as "Madame Karitas." ~35 performers per event. Quarterly at Idno. Featured Nobel laureate Sjon.

Askur Yggdrasils Maze (2016)

370 sqm outdoor labyrinth for Reykjavik Culture Night. 250 pallets, 4000 screws. 24 artists, 3,000 visitors.

Heima (2016)

An Icelandic Experience at Deptford Cinema, London. Sigur Ros screening, live music, art exhibition, Icelandic food.

The Reform Club Presents (2016)

Immersive journey for 25 guests, based on Around The World In 80 Days. With House of Q.

ABBA Voyage Launch (2021)

Local production for ABBA's global album launch livestream at Sky Lagoon, Reykjavik.

Of Monsters And Men / Fever Dream Day (2019)

Street performance with 8 masked dancers, hidden synchronized speakers, custom internet radio.

The Hidden People (2018-2022)

Aerial circus theatre with Hikapee. Icelandic folklore meets creative technology.
